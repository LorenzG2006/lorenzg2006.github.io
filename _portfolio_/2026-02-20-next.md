**mo-tion next** is an advanced event technology platform developed at mo-tion, a Vienna-based company specializing in interactive presentations, video mappings, and real-time sports graphics. The platform powers high-end visual solutions for live events, broadcasts, and permanent installations across Europe.

More information can be found on the official [mo-tion next](https://next.geyser.at).
